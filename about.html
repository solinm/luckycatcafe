about us

Lucky Cat Café opened its doors in Ottawa in 2022, born from a simple dream: to create a warm, welcoming space where coffee enthusiasts and cat lovers could come together. Inspired by the cat café phenomenon abroad, our founders wanted to offer something more than your average café—an opportunity for our feline residents to find their forever homes.

From the start, we partnered with local shelters to give adoptable cats a comfortable, cage-free environment where they can socialize and flourish. Visitors can enjoy freshly brewed coffee, decadent pastries, and most importantly, quality time with our charming whiskered companions. Whether it’s a peaceful afternoon cuddling with a sleepy tabby or an energetic play session with a curious kitten, every visit helps these cats build confidence and steal hearts.

Stop by, grab a latte, and settle in for some purr therapy. At Lucky Cat Café, you’ll find a place to relax, connect, and maybe even discover a new best friend. It’s our way of spreading warmth throughout the Ottawa community—one cup and one cuddle at a time.
