café rules

To ensure a safe and comfortable experience for our guests—both human and feline—we kindly ask that you follow these guidelines:

1. No Outside Pets
Please refrain from bringing any other animals into the café. Our resident cats are accustomed to their environment and each other.

2. Approach Gently
Allow the cats to come to you. Do not chase or pick them up without guidance from staff.

3. Mind Their Privacy
If a cat is sleeping or resting, avoid disturbing them. Cats need personal space and rest time.

4. No Flash Photography
Bright lights can startle our cats. Feel free to take photos, but please turn off the flash and keep noise to a minimum.

5. Keep Human Food to Yourself
Our cats follow a special diet; do not feed them from your plate or drinks. They have their own treats provided by staff.

6. Sanitize Hands
Use the provided sanitizer before interacting with the cats. This helps maintain a clean, healthy environment.

7. Gentle Voices, Calm Atmosphere
Loud noises or sudden movements can stress our cats, so please speak softly and move mindfully.

8. Supervise Children
Children are welcome but must be closely supervised by an adult at all times to ensure respectful, safe interactions with the cats.

By following these rules, you help us protect the well-being of our beloved feline residents—and create a peaceful, enjoyable setting for everyone. Thank you for your cooperation and understanding!
