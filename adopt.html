winston

2 y/o | male

Winston is the epitome of a calm companion—he’s equally content curling up next to you or surveying the room from a lofty perch. He greets new friends with a polite nuzzle, and while he may seem reserved at first, he blossoms once he recognizes a trustworthy hand. Offer him a cozy blanket to knead, and he’ll reward you with a soothing, steady purr that’s perfect for quiet evenings in.

luna

1 y/o | female

Don’t let her petite stature fool you—Luna is bursting with curiosity and charm. She’s a nimble climber who will perch herself high on a shelf just to watch the world below. Whether she spots a feather wand or a dangling string, she can’t resist pouncing into action. Luna also has a softer side: if you sit still long enough, she’ll come investigate and might curl up beside you.

mochi

2 y/o | female

Mochi is a spirited ball of energy who adores batting around toy mice and dashing after laser pointers. When she’s not on the hunt for her next play session, she’ll stretch out in a sunbeam for a well-deserved catnap. Though she’s always up for adventure, Mochi loves gentle cuddles at the end of the day and will melt into purrs the moment you scratch her chin.
