volunteer with us

Volunteering at Lucky Cat Café is a wonderful way to combine your love of cats with community service. We’re looking for enthusiastic individuals who are at least 18 years old, reliable, and able to commit to a regular weekly shift.

Responsibilities include feeding and cleaning, socializing with our feline residents, assisting visitors with basic cat-care guidance, and helping maintain a cozy, welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

To apply, please email your resume to meow@luckycatcafe.ca, along with a short note about why you’d be a great fit for our team. Once we review your application, we’ll reach out to schedule an orientation session, during which you’ll learn about our café procedures, meet our whiskered residents, and discover how your time and care can help these cats find their forever homes.

Join us, and become part of a supportive environment dedicated to sharing purrs, improving feline lives, and brightening the Ottawa community—one cuddle at a time.
